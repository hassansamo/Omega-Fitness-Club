about us

What We Do

Omega Fitness Club is a gym that helps you achieve your fitness dreams. We provide you with the best facilities whether you want to lose weight, gain muscle or improve your health.

body and mind

We support your mental health and well-being in a friendly environment, with music for an enjoyable workout.

healthy life

We promote lifelong healthy habits through nutrition advice, wellness tips, and resources for improved eating and sleep.

strategies

We evaluate your fitness level, goals, and interests to design a challenging and motivating program.

Equipment

We provide access to top equipment and machines, with professional trainers ensuring safe and effective use.

gym features

Weight Lifting

Our gym provides diverse equipment for strength and muscle building, including dumbbells, barbells, kettlebells, plates, punching bag, battle rope, benches, cables, and more. Our trainers ensure safe and effective lifting techniques.

Women-Only Time

Respecting and supporting our female members, we offer exclusive women-only gym hours for a comfortable and secure workout environment with like-minded women.

Cardiovascular Machines

Our gym offers various cardio machines, such as treadmills, bikes, elliptical, and more, to enhance endurance and heart health.

pricing plan
